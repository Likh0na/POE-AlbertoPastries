Our Story: A Family Tradition

Alberto's Pastries in Livorno is a renowned shop known for its exceptional Sfogliatelle, artistic decor, and commitment to quality. We pride ourselves on baking a variety of fresh pastries daily, ensuring our customers are always happy and satisfied.

Visit us and enjoy classic Italian treats, such as Cannolis, Croissants, Cream Buns, and much more. All our products are made by our founder, Magri Alberto, who has been passionate about baking since his childhood in Italy, inspired by his mother, who owned her own bakery.
